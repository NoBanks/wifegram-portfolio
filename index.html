How it works

  1. 01

    You build her Master File in 4 minutes

    Her name, pet names, what she's into, what she's carrying right now, the inside jokes only you two share, what she loves about you, and what to never mention. The specificity is the product. "Saturday morning decaf oat lattes from Cole's" beats "she likes coffee."

  2. 02

    Pick the vibe and when to send

    Encouragement. Humor. Romantic. Check-in. Morning, afternoon, night. Whatever fits her and fits your week. Cron does the remembering, you do the loving.

  3. 03

    You approve every single send

    15 minutes before each send, the bot DMs you the draft plus 4 image options. Tap Send 1, 2, 3, or 4. Or hit "More casual" / "Shorter" / "Add inside joke" / "More specific" / "Rewrite from scratch." Or skip the day entirely. Wifegram is never on autopilot. The ultimate executive assistant, but the executive still has to sign the letter.

  4. 04

    It lands in her chat as if from you

    Telegram delivery. Text plus the image you picked. No "sent via Wifegram" footer. No watermark. Just a thoughtful message from her husband, on a day he was supposedly buried at work.

Privacy

Mathematically invisible to everyone but you

App-level encryption

Her details are encrypted on the device before they ever hit storage. The file on disk is scrambled bytes. The key never sits next to the file.

Hashed do-not-mention list

The topics you tell it to never reference are stored as one-way fingerprints. Even insiders with full database access can't read the original topics.

No training, no resale, no sharing

Your wife's data is never used to train any model. Never sold. Never shared across accounts. Each account is row-level isolated. The buttons for every other floor don't even exist on the elevator panel.
